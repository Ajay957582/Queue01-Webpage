ABOUT

PURPOSE

In This Digital World ,it seems very odd, where people are standing in a long queue , for getting service of few minutes at some places like some famous hospitals , any famous restraunt , any crowded temple , any famous shop and many many more... We have seen many people give up there hours of precious time in standing in a queue unneccessary for 5 minutes of work . This is very strange !! isn't it ? So we have came with this website , to solve this problem and bring a revolution in the world in this feild . For this ,simply user have to make their profile on this website , and do search of registered places ,where they need to go and book a slot their online . By doing this simple process user will shortly recieve a message which shows the exact date and time of their turn , and hence user will get services without wasting hours standing in a queue
